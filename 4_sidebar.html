Scale UpCycle

STUNT

Developing artefacts from recycled plastic sheets to popularize circularity among youth.

RoleLead Product Designer
ClientStichting Stunt & The Upcycle
ToolsRhino 3D, CNC, User Testing, VCarve, Laser Cutter
Duration15 Weeks

From Waste to Wonder: Inclusive Design with Recycled Plastics

Part of the “Scale UpCycle” initiative — a collaboration between The Hague University of Applied Sciences and Stichting Stunt, which provides job opportunities for people reintegrating into the workforce and turns plastic waste into high-quality sheets. The brief: conceptualize and develop artefacts from 10 mm recycled HDPE sheets, enhancing a configurator that lets kids design their own toys and popularizes recycling through participation.

VESTIGE

“Ascend your space with seamless storage solutions, uniting strength and tranquility through modular design.”

Target AudienceMillennials & Gen Z
MaterialSustainable Multiplex Wood
Key PrinciplesDesign for Disassembly, Closed Loop
Dimensions710 × 400 × 2000 mm

Redefining furniture for the circular economy

80.2% of “fast furniture” ends up in landfills, driven by low-quality materials and inseparable components. VESTIGE bridges affordable fast furniture and premium eco-design: a modular system built for longevity, repairability and total disassembly, for a generation that values sustainability but lacks budget-friendly options.

Design for Disassembly

Every component can be separated, repaired, or recycled.

Material Honesty

Exposed joinery and natural finishes celebrate the sustainable materials.

Life-Cycle Adaptability

As users move or their needs change, Vestige adapts with them.

LocationNetherlands

A Modern Collective

The CPC Housing project explores the balance between privacy and community through a “Collective Private Commission” model where residents actively shape their living environment. Large open-plan communal areas encourage spontaneous interaction while maintaining high-quality private quarters; natural light and warm materials create an atmosphere both modern and homey.
